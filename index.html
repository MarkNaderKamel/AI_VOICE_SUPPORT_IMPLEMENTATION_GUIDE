How to Use This Demo

  1. Click the "Open AI Voice Support" button above
  2. Allow microphone access when prompted by your browser
  3. Click the orange microphone button in the modal
  4. Speak your question clearly (try: "What services do you offer?")
  5. Wait for the AI to process and respond
  6. The response will be displayed and spoken aloud

Important Note

This demo page is for frontend demonstration only. The AI functionality requires a properly configured backend with your Gemini API key. See the implementation guide for complete setup instructions.
